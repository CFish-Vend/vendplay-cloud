----------------------------------------
LESSONS LEARNED (APRIL 2026)
----------------------------------------

• Stripe webhooks can be delivered multiple times (including delayed retries)
• Duplicate protection MUST use event_id (not queue state)
• “Pending” checks do NOT prevent delayed duplicates
• Always verify actual system behavior before adding fixes
• One change at a time — avoid layered fixes without confirmation
